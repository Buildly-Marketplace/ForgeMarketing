# Brand Voice Guide

## Define Brand Voice

For each brand/product, document:
- short brand summary
- audience
- voice traits
- words/phrases to use
- words/phrases to avoid
- primary CTA style

Example voice traits:
- calm
- practical
- direct
- supportive
- lightly playful

## Use Templates Consistently

Start from content templates by platform and format:
- hook
- core message
- proof or example
- CTA

Templates improve consistency and reduce blank-page friction.

## Review AI Drafts

AI output is draft-only.

Review checklist:
- matches brand tone
- factual and compliant
- no spammy claims
- clear CTA
- platform-appropriate length
- includes alt text guidance when needed

Status progression:
- AI Draft
- Human Edited
- Approved
- Ready to Schedule

## Avoid Spammy Content

Do not use:
- fake urgency
- engagement bait loops
- mass-tagging unrelated accounts
- repetitive low-value copy
- unsupported claims

## Keep Content Human

- Add one real insight, story, or observation per post.
- Use plain language.
- Keep promises realistic.
- Prioritize trust over reach hacks.
