# Content Calendar Guide

## Plan a Week

1. Select one active campaign.
2. Pick 3 to 5 content pillars.
3. Create 5 to 10 content ideas.
4. Convert ideas into platform drafts.
5. Assign owners and due dates.
6. Reserve time for review and approval.

## Use Content Pillars

Pillars keep variety and prevent repetitive posting. Example pillars:
- Founder story
- Product feature
- Educational tip
- Community update
- User success
- Behind the scenes

## Track Due Dates

Use due dates for:
- idea drafting
- review
- approval
- scheduling
- posting
- analytics checks

If calendar UI is limited, use grouped list by date and status.

## Avoid Over-Posting

- Avoid posting too frequently on one platform when quality drops.
- Keep at least one high-quality post per platform cadence.
- Use weekly review to tune frequency.

## Keep Campaigns Organized

- Tie every idea to a campaign when possible.
- Use status labels consistently.
- Archive outdated ideas.
- Keep notes with links to assets and posted URLs.

## Recommended Labels

- Ideas
- Drafts
- Needs Review
- Approved
- Ready to Schedule
- Scheduled
- Posted
- Check Performance
- Manual Tasks
- Campaigns
- Assets
